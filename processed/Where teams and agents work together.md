## Claude Skills 101

Skills = instruction sets — Best practices that teach Claude how to do specific tasks well (docs, spreadsheets, presentations)

Agents = autonomous actors — Handle multi-step processes with planning, tool use, and adapting on the fly

Think recipes vs. chefs — Skills are recipes for consistent results; agents are chefs who plan and improvise

Use skills for reliable outputs — Pitch decks, PDFs, reports where you want proven patterns

Use agents for judgment calls — Research, debugging, anything requiring decisions and iteration
