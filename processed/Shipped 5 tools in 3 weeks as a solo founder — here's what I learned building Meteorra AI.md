Three weeks ago Meteorra AI had one tool and a homepage.

Today we have five:

**🔍 YouTube Analyser**  
Paste any YouTube URL — get a full metadata score, side-by-side optimisation and specific fixes in 30 seconds. Free, no signup.

**🥊 Competitor Insights**  
See why other videos in your niche are beating yours. Paste your URL, find top competitors, see exactly what they're doing differently with their metadata.

**💡 Video Idea Validator**  
Type your video idea before you film it. Get a discoverability score, 3 better angles, title formats and target audience. Saves hours of wasted filming.

**🖼️ Thumbnail Analyser**  
Get thumbnail direction before you design — then score your finished thumbnail before you upload. Know if it'll get clicks before the algorithm decides.

**📊 Generate Strategy**  
Full pre-publish content strategy — audience segments, regional opportunities, hashtag stacks, title formats.

**What else happened in 3 weeks:**

- Hit #1 Product of the Week on Fazier 🏆 with 80+ upvotes
- 6 competitor comparison pages ranking position 4-5 on Google
- Featured in AI in Plain English on Medium
- Listed on 12+ directories
- 100 X followers from zero
- 15 product signups
- PH relaunch planned July 22

**What I learned:**

**Ship fast, fix later.**  
Every tool had bugs on day one. None of them mattered as much as getting it live and getting feedback.

**Community feedback is product gold.**  
The Fazier comments gave us more actionable product insights than any user research session would have.

**SEO compounds faster than you think.**  
Six VS pages went live and started ranking within days. Organic traffic from "VidIQ alternatives" searches is already coming in.

**Replies beat posts on X.**  
Original posts with 100 followers get 50 impressions. Replies on big accounts get 500. Borrowed reach beats earned reach at this stage.

**The honest numbers:**  
No paid users yet. 15 signups. 100 X followers. But the foundation feels solid and the July 22 PH launch is the real test.

Free to try at [meteorra.ai](http://meteorra.ai/) — brutal feedback welcome as always 🙏
